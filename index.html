About Us

Auragen Technologies

Auragen Technologies is a smart energy startup bootstrapping operations out of Botswana, Sub Saharan Africa. We use dynamic & personalized advanced methods that translate into work-flow engines that are relevant to the region's needs. The described methods empower energy users by alloting them the requisite capital to achieve their energy access goals.

Read More
Sub-Saharan Africa is home to 1.35 billion inhabitants, with 6.9 ppl on average/household. This effectively means that the region is home to around 196 million homes & over half of the region's inhabitants lack basic energy access. Why you may ask? Answer: Processes Everything is a process, even energy access falls under this maxim, i.e. to say better process management makes it conducive to access energy, without these processes energy becomes inaccessible. Failure to develop better methods, processes etc. elicites the status quo the region is currently experiencing. Auragen Innovations as a startup looks to change this status quo through it's Hermes Digital Experience Platform that looks to correct the processes that have failed resolve the region's energy problems.
